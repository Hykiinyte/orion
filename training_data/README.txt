The \training_data\ folder is divided into distinct folders of data for training Orion. Here is the current standing of how to manipulate data for the Orion 1 model.

\text\ This folder is used for general text training. Use this to train the model for simply creating text.

\dialogue\ This folder is used for training the AI at dialogue. This teaches it how to be a chat bot and not just a predictor.

\code\ This folder is used to train the AI at coding.

\images\ This folder is used to train the AI at images. Currently not in use.
